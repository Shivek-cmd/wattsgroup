About Watts Group

A Canadian multi-vertical conglomerate built on purpose, innovation, and the belief that technology can unlock human potential at scale.

Our Story

From Vision to Venture

Watts Group Ltd was founded in Canada with a singular belief: that the most impactful companies are built at the intersection of technology, purpose, and community.

What began as a single AI consulting venture has grown into a multi-vertical conglomerate spanning eight companies across six industry sectors — from artificial intelligence and real estate to immigration, education, and entertainment.

Today, we operate across Canada, India, and the UAE, with a team of over 50 professionals united by a shared mission: to build companies that create real-world impact.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

2020

Foundation

Watts Group Ltd is incorporated in Canada with a vision to build a purpose-driven multi-vertical conglomerate.

2021

First Ventures Launch

Watts Technologies and Watts Immigration launch, establishing our presence in AI consulting and immigration services.

2022

Portfolio Expansion

Aifyze and Abroadiz join the portfolio, extending our reach into AI automation and study abroad consulting.

2023

Real Estate & Community

Watts Realty and Watts Community launch, adding real estate brokerage and CSR-driven education to the group.

2024

Full Ecosystem

Applyio and Fun-A-Boo complete the portfolio — eight companies across six verticals, operating in three countries.

2025

Scale & Impact

Watts Group enters its growth phase — scaling operations, expanding internationally, and deepening community impact.
